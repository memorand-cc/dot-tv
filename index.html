What is Portobello?

Portobello is the setup of a personal streaming service based on Stremio.

Stremio is a video player that can expand its capabilities with the use of extensions. Portobello simply sets up the appropriate extensions to provide a personal streaming service.

What devices are supported?

  • Android phones and tablets
  • Android TVs and Android TV devices
  • Google TVs and Google TV devices
  • Chromecast
  • Samsung TVs, 2019 onwards
  • LG TVs, from 2020 onwards
  • PCs running Windows, MacOS or Linux

Currently iOS devices (iPhone, iPad, Apple TV) are not supported, but are already in the testing phase, so it is expected that they will be supported later this year.

Can I download the content to watch it offline?

You can't download content, only stream it.

Can I share my account?

The account has no profiles, so it works with a single user. You can share it if you want, but keep in mind that using it from more than one place at a time might impact performance.

Can I use an existing Stremio account?

This service only works with Stremio accounts set up by Portobello.

Which personal data is stored?

Only the email address you use to make the payment is saved for as long as you use the service. Absolutely nothing else. If at any time you decide to stop using it, your email address will be removed from the database.

Portobello does not have access to or store any of the data that PayPal requests to make the payment, or any information about your use of the service with Stremio.

This website does not log your IP address or location and does not use any cookies.
